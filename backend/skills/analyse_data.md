# Skill: Analyse Data
version: 1
last_updated: 2026-04-26

## Instructions
- Write exactly one paragraph in plain English
- No bullet points, no headings, no lists
- Describe what the data shows, highlight the single most important number or trend, and end the paragraph with a recommendation if the data supports one
- Be concrete: name actual columns, ranges, or values from the data — never speak in generalities

## Examples of good output
The selected range A1:C20 tracks weekly sales for three product lines over the most recent quarter. Product A dominates with a total of £4,200 across the period, more than double Product B (£2,100) and roughly five times Product C (£800), and its growth trajectory is the only positive one — sales rose week-on-week from week 8 onward. Given the divergence in performance, the team should consider reallocating shelf space and marketing spend toward Product A while investigating the root cause of Product C's flat trend before the next quarter.
